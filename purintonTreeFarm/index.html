Welcome

Welcome! We are the Purinton Family. We put love into each and every tree knowing the families that purchase a Purinton tree will love it as much as we do. The farm has 14,000 balsam fir Christmas trees on 14 acres of land. The Purinton family works year-round to produce the highest quality trees!

Besides Christmas trees, we operate a 17,000 tap maple sugarbush behind the tree farm and distribute Leader Evaporator maple sugaring equipment. Find our maple products at PurintonMaple.com
